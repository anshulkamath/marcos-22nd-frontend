Marcos' Advent Calendar!

Welcome!

You, Marcos David Acosta, probably have a few questions (if not, you should). First, why am I addressing you like I would an audience? Second, why didn't you have to solve an incredibly elaborate and well-crafted puzzle to get to this page? And, third, why am I sending this to you on a day that is verifiably not your birthday?

You see, this is no ordinary Marcos birthday present. I have gone through the extended toil of making sure that this birthday spectacular is one for the ages. Below, you will be faced with a series of Herculean tasks designed to make you fail. If you somehow find a way to pass all these tests, you will (probably) be rewarded accordingly.

Below, you will notice that there is a calendar enumerating all the days between June 1, 2023 and June 14, 2023. It should be noted, however, that this is no ordinary calendar. No, no, no. This is an ~advent~ calendar. Don't worry, though. I have been informed that even the smallest advent calendar can leave you shaking in your boots. Hence, I have added some extra protection for both of our sakes - you can only continue to the next day of the calendar under two conditions:
  1. You have successfully completed the previous challenge and submitted the code, and
  2. It has passed 12 am (EST) on the given day.
